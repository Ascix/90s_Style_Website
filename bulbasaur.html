Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a small, quadrupedal amphibian Pok√©mon that has blue-green skin with darker patches. It has red eyes with white pupils, pointed, ear-like structures on top of its head, and a short, blunt snout with a wide mouth. On Bulbasaur's back is a green plant bulb, which is grown from a seed planted there at birth.
